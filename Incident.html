Since the majority of shooting incidents have less than 10 victims per incident, the total shootings were grouped into small and large victim counts. There also were many areas that had multiple occurences of mass shootings per county. The focus on the amount of occureneces were split into single incident and multi-incident categories for further investigation.

The counties where incidents are recurring have lower fatality rates for higher incident counts. There are some counties that have had more than 20 mass shootings, and two where there are more than 40. The majority recurrences are in areas that have had less than 10 incidents per county. For the single incident occurences, the areas with higher victim counts have less than a 40% fatality rate. The counties with the higher fatality rates tend to be rural or suburban.
